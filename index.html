Decoder Information

This decoder implements:

  • GRIB2 Edition 2 parsing
  • Section 0-7 parsing
  • Simple packing (type 0) support
  • Constant value packing (type 40)
  • IEEE 754 single precision packing (type 254)
  • Missing data handling (type 255)
